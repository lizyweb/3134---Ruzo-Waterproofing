Our Services

Comprehensive Waterproofing Solutions for Every Need

At Ruzo Waterproofing, we provide tailored solutions to protect your property from water damage. Our services range from foundation waterproofing to specialized coatings for rooftops and basements, ensuring durability and peace of mind.

Roof Waterproofing

Protect your home from leaks and weather damage with our high-quality roof waterproofing solutions.Designed to withstand extreme conditions, our roof coatings provide long-lasting protection.

Basement Waterproofing

Our advanced basement waterproofing keeps your lower levels dry and safe from water infiltration. We use durable materials and techniques to prevent leaks and ensure a moisture-free space.

Bathroom & Kitchen Waterproofing

Protect high-moisture areas like bathrooms and kitchens with our targeted waterproofing solutions. Our services prevent water seepage and extend the longevity of your interior spaces.

External Wall Waterproofing

Protect your exterior walls from water damage and mold growth with our effective waterproofing solutions.We apply advanced coatings that ensure long-lasting durability and aesthetic appeal.

Foundation Waterproofing

Ensure the integrity of your building's foundation with our specialized waterproofing techniques.Our solutions prevent moisture penetration and protect against structural damage.

Waterproofing Applicators

Our team of certified waterproofing applicators specializes in protecting your property from water damage. Using advanced techniques and high-quality materials, we ensure that your exterior surfaces are safeguarded against moisture, mold, and degradation.

Construction Chemicals

Our range of construction chemicals is designed to enhance the durability and performance of your structures. From waterproofing agents to sealants, we provide solutions that ensure the integrity of your construction in high-moisture areas like bathrooms and kitchens.

Epoxy Grouting

Our expert team specializes in epoxy grouting, providing long-lasting solutions to reinforce and seal joints, cracks, and voids in various structures. With advanced materials and techniques, we enhance the durability and resilience of surfaces against water, chemicals, and abrasion.

Heat & Waterproof Coating for Floors

Our heat and waterproof coating solutions are specially formulated to protect your floors from extreme temperatures and moisture. Ideal for terraces, balconies, and high-exposure areas, these coatings ensure durability and comfort by minimizing heat absorption and preventing water damage.

Bitumen Membrane Sheet

Our team provides professional installation of bitumen membrane sheets, a reliable solution for waterproofing and protecting surfaces from moisture and weathering. Ideal for flat roofs, basements, and other exposed surfaces, our high-quality bitumen sheets enhance durability and offer excellent resistance to extreme weather.

Construction Waterproofing Solutions

Our construction waterproofing solutions are designed to protect your structures from water intrusion and damage. Whether it’s for basements, foundations, or exterior walls, our advanced systems ensure long-lasting durability and safeguard your property against moisture-related issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of waterproofing services do you offer?

    Ruzo Waterproofing provides a wide range of waterproofing solutions, including foundation waterproofing, basement waterproofing, roof waterproofing, bathroom and kitchen waterproofing, and external wall waterproofing.

  • How do I know if my property needs waterproofing?

    Signs that your property may need waterproofing include visible leaks, damp walls, mold growth, unpleasant odors, or standing water. If you notice any of these signs, it’s advisable to have a professional assessment to determine the best waterproofing solution.

  • What makes Ruzo Waterproofing different from other companies?

    We stand out due to our expertise, advanced waterproofing formulations, eco-friendly solutions, customized approach, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Our team’s extensive experience ensures reliable, high-quality results on every project.

  • Are your waterproofing solutions environmentally friendly?

    Yes, Ruzo Waterproofing offers eco-friendly options with low VOC (volatile organic compounds) content. We are committed to using sustainable materials and methods to protect both your property and the environment.

  • How long does waterproofing last?

    The longevity of waterproofing depends on the product and the environmental conditions, but our high-quality waterproofing solutions typically last 10–20 years with proper maintenance. We also offer warranties for added peace of mind.

  • Is waterproofing necessary for new buildings?

    Yes, waterproofing is a proactive measure that helps prevent future water damage and structural issues. Installing waterproofing in new constructions is a wise investment that extends the life and integrity of the building.

  • How long does a waterproofing project take?

    The duration of a waterproofing project depends on the scope and complexity of the work. Most residential projects are completed within a few days, while larger or more complex jobs may take longer. We’ll provide a clear timeline during the consultation phase.
