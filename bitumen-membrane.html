Bitumen Membrane Sheets

Our expert team specializes in the application of bitumen membrane sheets, providing effective waterproofing solutions for various structures. Designed to resist moisture and extreme weather conditions, our membranes ensure the longevity and durability of your roofs and surfaces.

Key Features
  • Durable Protection: Bitumen membranes offer strong resistance against water infiltration, ensuring long-term protection for your property.
  • Weather Resistance: These sheets are engineered to withstand harsh weather conditions, including heavy rain and UV exposure.
  • Flexible Application: Suitable for a variety of surfaces, including flat and sloped roofs, basements, and foundations.
  • Professional Installation: Our certified applicators use best practices to guarantee proper installation and maximum effectiveness of the membrane system.


  • Applications
  • Benefits
  • Application Guidelines
  • Technical Specifications

Applications

  • Roof Waterproofing: Ideal for flat and low-slope roofs, providing an effective barrier against rain and water pooling.

  • Basement Protection: Essential for below-grade applications to prevent moisture ingress and damage.

  • Foundation Sealing: Protects foundations from water damage, enhancing structural stability.

  • Parking Structures: Provides a durable waterproofing solution for parking decks and garages to prevent water penetration.

Benefits

  • Exceptional Waterproofing: Bitumen membranes create a strong barrier that effectively prevents water infiltration.

  • UV Resistance: These membranes are designed to withstand UV radiation, prolonging their lifespan.

  • Cost-Effective Solution: Durable and long-lasting, they reduce the need for frequent repairs and replacements.

  • Easy Installation: Lightweight and flexible, making them easy to handle and apply on various surfaces.

Application Guidelines

  • Surface Preparation: Ensure surfaces are clean, dry, and free of any loose materials before application.

  • Temperature Considerations: Apply membranes when temperatures are between 5°C and 30°C for optimal adhesion.

  • Proper Overlapping: Ensure overlaps are at least 10 cm to provide adequate waterproofing coverage.

  • Post-Installation Checks: Inspect the membrane after installation to confirm there are no gaps or bubbles.

Technical Specifications

  • Thickness: Typical thickness ranges from 3mm to 5mm, providing adequate protection and flexibility.

  • Adhesive Properties: Ensure compatibility with both hot and cold adhesive systems for versatile applications.

  • Water Vapor Transmission: Bitumen membranes have a low water vapor transmission rate, enhancing overall performance.

  • Fire Resistance: Products should meet local fire resistance standards to enhance safety in various applications.
